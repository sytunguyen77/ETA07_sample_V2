    Master the Art of a Perfect Listing

    Here are six ways to get your product noticed and sold.

    Craft a Powerful Title

    • • We'll suggest popular search terms buyers are using. Make sure to include these in your title.
    • • Avoid using all capital letters. Instead, focus on specific details like brand, model, size, and color.

    Capture High-Quality Photos

    • • Take clear photos from multiple angles in good lighting, and include any imperfections so buyers know exactly what to expect.
    • • Using the ETA07 app, you can easily edit your photos and create a clean, white background.

    Choose the Right Category

    • • If you're not sure which category your item fits into, we have a tool that will choose it for you.
    • • If you provide the wrong category, we will update this for you so it can reach the right buyer.

    Write Detailed Descriptions

    • • Include key details like condition, dimensions, and any unique features. Be honest about any flaws.
    • • Our AI writing assistant can help you create compelling descriptions that convert browsers into buyers.

    Set Competitive Prices

    • • Our pricing tool analyzes similar listings to suggest the optimal price for a quick sale.
    • • Consider offering bundle deals or accepting offers to attract more potential buyers.

    Ship with Confidence

    • • Choose from our discounted shipping options or offer local pickup for larger items.
    • • Track your shipments and communicate with buyers through our secure messaging system.
